# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Young adults, church leaders, donors, and partners across Asia and beyond — discovering the Arise Asia missions movement and deciding how to engage (attend the summit, give financially, volunteer, pray, or bring the movement to their country).

## Product Purpose

Arise Asia is a missions movement (launched 2023) that inspires young people across Asia to take the gospel where it has never been heard. The site recruits, informs, and converts visitors into participants and mission-senders. By 2033 the goal is 100,000 young people from 500 cities and 50 countries mobilized.

## Brand Personality

**Bold · Urgent · Joyful**

Voice is confident and direct, not salesy or sentimental. Urgency comes from the countdown and the scale of the vision, not from pressure tactics. Joy carries the tone — the movement's tagline is "For The Joy".

## Anti-references

No specific anti-references. Avoid generic corporate/SaaS design and overly traditional church aesthetic — the movement is youth-forward, global, and modern without being trendy.

## Design Principles

1. **Movement, not a website** — Every element drives toward action (attend, give, volunteer, pray). Content recruits; chrome doesn't.
2. **Joyful urgency** — Countdowns, bold colors, and imperative CTAs create urgency; icon flourishes, warm palette, and "For The Joy" brandline carry joy.
3. **Pan-Asian first** — Three locales from day one (en/id/zh-TW). Design must feel culturally resonant across Asia, not Western with translated labels.
4. **Content-driven hierarchy** — Big typography (Unbounded display), bold color blocking, and spacious layout carry the message before any interface element.
5. **Accessible boldness** — WCAG AA contrast; high-contrast colors ensure the bold palette reaches everyone.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Standard contrast ratios, keyboard navigation where interactive, reduced-motion support for animated elements.
